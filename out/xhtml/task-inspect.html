To inspect the meter:

  1. Log into SalesLogix and open the RMA number listed on the paperwork.
  2. Inspect the enclosure of the M800 meter and check the appearance of the front panel, looking for any visible damage.
  3. Use the Phillips-head screwdriver to loosen the 4 screws on the front panel to allow the top to lift open.
  4. Inspect the visible boards and connectors. Look for any broken or damaged connectors, cracked surfaces, or exposed wires.
  5. If the customer did not send a usable power cord, attach one. Power the M800 and watch to make sure it turns on and progresses through the start-up screens until the measurement screen displays.
  6. If the customer sent a patch cord, disconnect the leads by pushing the orange spring clips with the small flat-head screwdriver one at a time while pulling gently on each lead.
  7. Record the details of any observed damage in SalesLogix. If the M800 looks to be in good condition, put in the notes that the equipment looks fine.
