Meter Calibration Overview

This entry describes the major steps in any conductivity meter recalibration.

Most industries require periodic recalibration of key instruments. The periods are sometimes determined by external regulations.

To recalibrate a meter:

  1. Log in the meter.
  2. Inspect the meter.
  3. Record the meter's setup.
  4. Perform the meter's recalibration.
  5. Update the records.
  6. Restore the meter's setup.
